    ABOUT US 

    We are thrilled to announce the highly anticipated 4th season of HACK THE MOUNTAINS! Building upon the tremendous success of our previous hackathons, this season promises even greater opportunities for participants. This 36-hour long event will bring together talented individuals from around the world.

     

    In a major leap forward, we are excited to introduce a hybrid format again this Year. This means that participants can join us both online and offline, creating a truly unique and dynamic experience. With this approach, we are expecting a whole new level of excitement, adventures, and challenges.

     

    Get ready to embark on an incredible journey of innovation, collaboration, and creativity. HACK THE MOUNTAINS Season 4 is set to surpass all expectations, and we can't wait to see the amazing solutions and ideas that will emerge from this global gathering of talented individuals.
